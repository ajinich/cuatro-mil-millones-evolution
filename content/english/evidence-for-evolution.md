# Evidence for Evolution

## Main Argument

**"It is a misconception to believe that the only evidence for evolution involves fossils."** The site emphasizes that evolution rests on diverse supporting evidence beyond paleontology.

## Types of Evidence

The page identifies six categories of evolutionary support:

1. **Anatomical** - Structural similarities among different animals
2. **Genetic** - DNA composition similarities between species
3. **Molecular** - Protein similarities in living organisms
4. **Geographic** - Species distribution patterns across locations
5. **Geological** - Fossil records and rock layers
6. **Observable** - "Hundreds of examples of evolution happening today"

## Scientific Robustness

**"Few scientific theories are as robust as evolution."** The site positions evolution as exceptionally well-grounded in scientific evidence, contrasting with common misconceptions that limit evolutionary proof to fossils alone.

## Alternative Evidence Sources

Beyond fossils, evidence includes:
- Animal anatomical resemblance
- Geographic species location patterns
- Protein similarity across living organisms
- DNA composition in related species

## Educational Purpose

The website serves as an educational resource presenting evolution through six distinct evidence categories, each demonstrating the comprehensive scientific support for evolutionary theory.
