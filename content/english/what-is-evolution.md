# What is Evolution?

## Main Definition

**"Evolution is descent with modification"**
- Charles Darwin

## Core Mechanisms

### Natural Selection
"Reproduction does not happen at random. In any given population, there will always be better adapted individuals that will leave more offspring than others." Organisms survive due to environmental adaptation rather than chance, possessing traits that provide reproductive advantage.

### Heritable Variation
Genetic differences arise randomly within populations. Inheritance of specific traits depends on chance, and traits may undergo random modifications called mutations.

## Key Concepts Explained

Evolution represents an **accumulative process** combining:
- Heritable genetic variation
- Reproductive success of adapted individuals
- Sufficient time for consequences to manifest
