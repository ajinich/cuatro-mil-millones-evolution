# Cuatro Mil Millones de Años: Evolución - Archive

## 📚 About This Repository

This repository preserves the educational content from **4milmillones.org**, a comprehensive Spanish-English educational website about biological evolution created by **María Garza-Jinich**. The original website was planned to go offline, so this archive ensures that this valuable educational resource remains accessible to students, educators, and anyone interested in understanding evolution.

## 🌍 What is "4 Mil Millones de Años"?

"4 Mil Millones de Años" (4 Billion Years) is an educational project that explains **biological evolution across 4.6 billion years of Earth's history**. The content is based on the book "Breve Historia de 4 Mil Millones de Años: Entendiendo a Darwin" (Brief History of 4 Billion Years: Understanding Darwin).

### Key Features:
- **Bilingual content** in Spanish and English
- **Comprehensive coverage** from basic evolutionary concepts to detailed species histories
- **Scientific accuracy** with extensive evidence-based explanations
- **Accessible explanations** designed for general audiences
- **Visual elements** including the "Tree of Life" illustrations

## 📖 Main Topics Covered

1. **¿Qué es evolución?** / **What is Evolution?**
   - Core definitions and Darwin's principles
   - Fundamental concepts of descent with modification

2. **Cómo funciona la evolución** / **How Evolution Works**
   - Natural selection mechanisms
   - Genetic variation, mutations, and heritability
   - Species formation and adaptive radiation

3. **Fechas evolutivas** / **Evolutionary Timeline**
   - 4.6 billion years of Earth's history mapped to a 12-hour clock
   - Key milestones from first life to human evolution

4. **Evidencias para la evolución** / **Evidence for Evolution**
   - Anatomical, geological, geographic, molecular, and contemporary evidence
   - Scientific robustness of evolutionary theory

5. **Evolución de primates** / **Primate Evolution**
   - Detailed primate lineage and characteristics
   - Geographic distribution and migration patterns

6. **Evolución humana** / **Human Evolution**
   - *Homo sapiens* evolution in Africa
   - Timeline from early hominids to modern humans

7. **Errores conceptuales** / **Common Misconceptions**
   - 20 major myths and errors about evolution addressed
   - Scientific vs. philosophical misconceptions
